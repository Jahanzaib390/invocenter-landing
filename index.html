FEATURES

Everything you need to
run your billing.

A complete invoicing toolkit — from creation to payment tracking, reporting, and beyond.

Smart Invoice Builder

Create beautiful, itemized invoices with automatic tax calculation, discounts, and currency formatting. Every amount stored in minor units for absolute precision.

Customer Management

Maintain a full customer database with contact details, invoice history, and lifetime value tracking. Import/export with ease.

Payment Tracking

Track payments with status, method, receipt numbers, and timestamps. See outstanding balances at a glance with real-time dashboard updates.

Dual Storage Engine

Choose between local SQLite for complete offline privacy, or Supabase cloud for real-time sync, auth, and multi-device access. Switch anytime.

PDF & Email Delivery

Generate professional PDF invoices and receipts. Send directly via SMTP with templated email bodies and PDF attachments — all from the app.

Analytics & Reporting

Interactive dashboards with revenue trends, customer analytics, and visual charts. Export reports to JSON and CSV for further analysis.

Enterprise Security

Local encryption for credentials, optional PIN-protected public invoice links with expiry and access logging. Role-based access for teams.

Recurring Invoices

Set up automated recurring billing with deduplication logic and scheduling. Works client-side or server-side with Supabase Edge Functions.

White-Label Ready

Full branding customization — logos, colors, company identity, and public invoice URLs. Deploy as your own product for your clients.

FAQ

Frequently asked
questions.

Is my data private?

Yes. By default, all your data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. Nothing leaves your computer unless you opt into Cloud Sync.

Does it need an internet connection?

No. InvoCenter works fully offline. You only need the internet to activate a license key or use the optional Cloud Sync feature.

Can I use it on multiple PCs?

Yes. Pro Desktop and Cloud Sync licenses allow activation on up to 3 devices with the same license key.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

Free gives you 5 invoices/month with a small "Made with InvoCenter" watermark on PDFs. Pro removes the watermark, unlocks unlimited invoices, recurring billing, email delivery, and public invoice links.

Is Cloud Sync a subscription?

Yes, Cloud Sync is $49/year. It adds real-time sync powered by Supabase so you can access your invoices from any device. Pro Desktop is a one-time purchase with no recurring charges.

How do I activate my license?

After purchasing, you'll receive a license key. Open InvoCenter → Settings → License & Plan → paste your key → Activate. That's it.
